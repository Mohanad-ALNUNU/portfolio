2023 - Films Website

Created a stylish and interactive film showcase app using Flutter, featuring a user-friendly interface for discovering and exploring films.

The app employs the MVC (Model-View-Controller) pattern to ensure a clear separation of concerns, enhancing maintainability and scalability of the codebase.

With a focus on clean code, the project follows industry best practices to ensure readability, modularity, and ease of collaboration among developers.

The source code for the project is available at: https://yip.su/films_app

The app showcases films in a visually appealing manner, providing users with the ability to search, browse, and explore film details seamlessly.
